## Editorial rules for the routine

- Rank by technical significance, not funding news or hype.
- Prefer primary sources (the paper, the release notes) over coverage of them.
- If a story is methodologically new (architecture, training method, agentic pattern), flag it as a deep-dive candidate at the end of the digest.
